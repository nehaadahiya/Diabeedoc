About Us

Diabeedoc is dedicated to helping diabetic patients manage their diets effectively through personalized recommendations and community support.A web-based food recommendation system for diabetic patients. The project integrates a machine learning model (Random Forest) to evaluate the healthiness of food items based on a provided dataset.The system accepts a food item input from the user. It matches the input to a dataset containing nutritional information (carbs, protein, fat, fiber), calories per 100 grams, and glycemic index (GI). Using a Random Forest model, the system evaluates if the food is healthy or not for diabetic patients, returning the recommendation as "Recommended" or "Not Recommended." The results display the glycemic index and calories per 100 grams of the queried food item.
